Mount Rinjani Open Trip can be a solution for those of you who don't have friends to climb Mount Rinjani in Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara. Mount Rinjani, located in Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara, is one of the mountains included in the list of the Seven Summits of Indonesia or the 7 highest peaks in Indonesia. Mount Rinjani is the most popular mountain for climbers because of its unique nature. In Rinjani there is the blue Segara Anak Lake and there is Mount Barujari in the middle, it looks very beautiful from above Plawangan Campsite and from below Lake Campsite. There is also hot water that we can enjoy while at the camp at Lake Segara Anak. To make it easier for climbers to visit Mount Rinjani, Indonesiatrip provides an Open Trip Mount Rinjani package 4 days 3 nights. On this open trip, you don't have to bother carrying climbing equipment and weights during the climb, just bring the personal items you want to bring. Because our team will serve all your needs during the Mount Rinjani Open Trip activities.
