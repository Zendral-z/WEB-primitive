Mount Sindoro (commonly called Sindara or Sundoro) has a height of 3,150 meters above sea level (MDPL). This mountain is an active volcano located in Central Java. The closest city to this mountain is the city of Temanggung. Mount Sindoro is located side by side with Mount Sumbing, therefore Sindoro is often called its twin Mount Sumbing. From the mountain, you can clearly see the peak of Sikunir Dieng. The history of the eruptions of Mount Sindoro that have occurred, mostly mild to moderate types (phreatic eruptions). Forests in the Mount Sindoro area are of the type of Bukit Dipterocarp forest, Upper Dipterocarp forest, Montane forest, and Ericaceous Forest or mountain forest. Mount Sindoro has two climbing routes that are quite popular among climbers. The two routes are via Kledung and Sikatok. Theater Adventure generally uses the route via Kledung. Well, one of the uniqueness of Mount Sindoro is its peak. The peak of Mount Sindoro is quite different from other high mountain peaks on the island of Java, which is that it has a very broad peak, it can even be estimated to be several times the size of a football stadium! This is what makes the peak of Mount Sindoro one of the favorite camping grounds for climbers.
