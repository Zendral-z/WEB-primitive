Mount Bromo as a Charming Tourist Attraction Mount Bromo is a tourist attraction located in East Java. Bromo is an active volcano with an altitude of approximately 2,392 meters above sea level, because it is still an active volcano that makes Bromo a unique and charming tourist spot. This mountain has a crater with a diameter of about 800 meters in the north-south part, for the east-west it has a diameter of about 600 meters, with a dangerous center area of ​​​​the crater in the form of a circle with a radius of about 4 km. Mount Bromo according to its location According to its territory, Mount Bromo is a volcano that is part of the Tengger Mountains National Park, Semeru. However, if viewed according to its administrative area, Bromo is located in 4 different areas, namely Malang Regency, Lumajang Regency, Pasuruan Regency and the last is Probolinggo Regency. To get to Mount Bromo you can use various means of transportation such as buses, trains, or planes. With these various means of transportation, you can go directly to the city of Malang and continue the journey to Mount Bromo Malang with travel agents or various public transportations that are widely available.
